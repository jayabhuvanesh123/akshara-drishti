About the Project

About Akshara-Drishti

Across temples, monuments, and archaeological sites, thousands of ancient inscriptions preserve valuable knowledge about culture, governance, religion, and society. However, many of these scripts remain inaccessible to modern readers.

Akshara-Drishti aims to bridge this gap using artificial intelligence. The system analyzes images of inscriptions and identifies script patterns, helping users understand the meaning and historical significance of these ancient texts.

The project combines AI technologies with historical research to create a platform that supports students, researchers, historians, and cultural enthusiasts. Our goal is to make ancient knowledge accessible to everyone through the power of modern technology.
