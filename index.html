Akshara-Drishti

AI Powered Indic Script Identification

Akshara-Drishti is a research project that explores the use of artificial intelligence to identify ancient Indic scripts from images of inscriptions found on monuments, temples, and archaeological artifacts.

Capabilities

What Makes It Unique

01

Script Recognition

Identify ancient writing systems like Brahmi, Tamil, Grantha, and Devanagari from a single photograph.

02

Image Restoration

Digital preprocessing restores weathered inscriptions using CLAHE, edge enhancement, and noise reduction.

03

Confidence Scoring

Each prediction comes with a confidence metric, helping researchers assess the reliability of the classification.
